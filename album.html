    • Tutorial and Tips

    • Never used a disposable camera? Watch this video to get started.
    • ** Always always turn on the flash!
    • ** Don't cover the lens or flash with your fingers!
    • ** Wind the film before each shot
    • ** Aim and Shoot! Proper distance is ~2 arms away from your target
